OKBK ATC — Phase 1 Sandbox

Open this page in the Meta Quest 3 browser, then tap START AR.

The tabletop spawns ~0.7 m in front of you at coffee-table height. Pinch an aircraft to read its flight strip; pinch again to dismiss. Pinch the table and drag to move it. Pinch with both hands on the table to scale + rotate. On desktop, drag to orbit.

Try: ?snapshot=okbk_today.json for today's curated 14:00 LT schedule · ?snapshot=okbk_live.json for live regional traffic (when fresh) · ?snapshot=example.json for 31 Dec 2025 20:00 LT.
